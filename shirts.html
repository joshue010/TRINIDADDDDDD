DWALHOLMS

Dwalholms is a local brand from the thoughts of a guy inside. Knowing that life is tough, the owner depicts positive messages through the designs of his shirts. This clothing brand is established online.


STRAP

Strap is a cebuano-estblished local brand that showcases the cebuano side of skateboarding. This brand includes homegrown talents that contributes to the filipino-pride, namely: Margielyn Didal and Motic Panugalinog. The brand features quality products such as footwear, skate and work pants, t-shirts and many more. They are located at Juana Osmena Extension, Cebu City.


VANITY

Vanity is a brand that showcases the creativity of the post-modern youth. Their styles originate from famous symbols such as the japan flag and the all-seeing eye concept.
